OUR SERVICES

  • High-Tech Hydroponics Agriculture

    Hydroponic agriculture is a farming method whereby plants grow by placing the roots in nutrients instead of soil. This method uses 10 times less water and the plants growth rate is %30 higher therefore increasing the plants production rate. All this is done by utilising the same amount of space.

  • Food Safety Responsibility

    Hydroponic farming helps the farmer control the plants’ environment by growing them in greenhouses which guarantees plants ripeness and wellness which makes it safer and more delicious for our customers to eat.

  • The Future of Al Mallouhi

    Since our current company is a supplier and a main seller for many big businesses and customers all over the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with our high production rate and success, we are looking to expand internationally while exploring more advanced and enhanced farming techniques to ensure plant ripeness when exported.
